Starting the Game

When you enter the main page you will already have a random maze generated for you. However if you wish to generate a new maze then just click the generate button.The timer for the game will begin when you either press the start button or start moving the black square.

How to play

The game is simple. The player is represented by the black square and the exit in the maze is represented by the red square. Your goal is to move the black square to the red square as fast as possiable. To move the black square the W,S,A,D keys are needed. W is up. A is left. S is down. D is right.

Scoreboard

the scoreboard is a dark blue box dislplayed at the right of the screen. Here is where you will find your time for the current maze being solved. The record for the fastest time a puzzle has ever been solved is also displayed here.
